Frequently Asked Questions

The calories in a Drumstick® vary by size and flavor. We recommend checking the label for the most current information before purchasing and consuming a product.

Because we're all about creating drool-worthy cones that are delicious from the first bite all the way down to the last bite, whether you eat our chocolatey 'nugget' first or last.

The Parker Brothers (I.C. and J.T. Parker) created the Drumstick® sundae cone in Fort Worth, Texas in 1928. Read more about our journey in the 'Our Story' section.

Most Drumstick® cones are kosher. The Drumstick® products that are Kosher Certified have the OU-D certification symbol noted on its packaging.

All Drumstick® raw dairy products (skim milk, whole milk and cream) are pasteurized in our facilities using the high temperature short time method.

Drumstick® does not currently produce a gluten free or non-dairy product.

Drumstick® snacks do not contain eggs.

Drumstick® snacks have a shelf life of 9 months from date of purchase.

At Drumstick®, we always label the nine major food allergens, including peanuts, on our package by their common name. If an allergen does not appear in the ingredient statement, then it has not been added to the product. If the product does not contain one of the known nine allergens, but is produced in a facility or on equipment that has potential for cross contact, we add at the end of the ingredient listing that it "May Contain" the allergen. We recommend to always check the label for the most current information before purchasing and consuming a product.

Drumstick® cones are topped with roasted peanuts. However, we also offer Drumstick® cones without added peanuts.

Drumstick® snacks are found at most grocery and convenience stores across the country. You can use our online Where To Buy tool to locate a store near you. You can also speak to the store manager of your favorite grocery store and request they carry your favorite flavor.

Store managers are in charge of what products they offer. If you'd like your favorite Drumstick® product in stock at your local grocery store, then we recommend letting your store manager know.

If you have to ask, you already know the answer. Have another Drumstick®.
