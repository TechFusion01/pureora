Frequently Asked QuestIons

We’re still the same company as Dreyer's and Edy's, but we decided to let the Outshine® brand live on its own. By choosing Outshine® as our name, we honor people like you who love our products and strive to ‘shine brighter’ in every aspect of their lives.

When in doubt, look for the label. Our “no GMO ingredients” icon on the front of our packaging identifies which flavors of Outshine® frozen snacks do not contain GMO ingredients. For more information on the Nestlé no GMO ingredients verification process, check out http://www.Nestléusa.com/about-us/gmo.

We're always trying to give you a better choice when it comes to picking your next snack. That's why we're offering you products made with no GMO ingredients, so you can choose how to Snack Brighter®.

With the exception of Outshine Creamy Coconut, Outshine Fruit Bars do not contain dairy.

Yes, all of our Outshine® frozen snacks are gluten free. Look for the gluten-free marking on all of our packaging.

Some of our Outshine® frozen snacks contain allergens. For these products, we always list the major food allergens in a “contains” statement after the ingredient statement. 

Yes, all of our Outshine® frozen snacks are Kosher OU-D certified. Look for the Kosher OU-D certified marking on all of our packaging.

Yes, all of our Outshine® products go through a pasteurization process that involves using appropriate temperatures and time during manufacturing to make sure all of our frozen snacks are safe for consumption.

At Outshine®, we always label the eight major food allergens, including nuts, on our package by their common name. If an allergen does not appear in the ingredient statement, then it has not been added to the product. We recommend to always check the label for the most current information before purchasing and/or consuming a product.

We clean our lines to ensure that no cross-contact is made with allergens that are not listed on the packaging, and we validate our cleaning procedure to make sure that it's effective.

Outshine® offers a variety of products that contain added sugar, as well as products that don't. 

The calorie count in Outshine® frozen fruit bars vary by flavor. We recommend to always check the label for the most current information before purchasing and/or consuming a product.

The carbohydrate count in Outshine® frozen fruit bars vary by flavor. We recommend to always check the label for the most current information before purchasing and/or consuming a product.

Potassium levels do vary between Outshine® products. In general, it can range from 1mg to 127mg per bar. 

Outshine® offers several refreshing frozen fruit bar flavors to choose from. Some of our most popular ones include, Strawberry, Creamy Coconut, Lime, and Grape. 

Outshine® frozen fruit bars are available at retailers nationwide. Use our “Where to Buy” feature to find stores near you.

All Outshine® frozen Fruit Bars are made with real fruit or fruit juice and contain at least 10% of your Daily Value of Vitamin C*. The bars contain no artificial colors or flavors** and no high fructose corn syrup, and are fat free*.  *Except Creamy Coconut Fruit Bars  **Added colors from natural sources

Our Outshine® No Sugar Added Fruit Bars have no added sugar, but we do use other sweeteners to give you the refreshingly delicious taste you love! You can view the full ingredient lists on our packaging.
