Our Fruit Bars

We use only the best ingredients because we believe in nutrition labels with words you can pronounce and the goodness of real fruit, juice and milk. For whenever you want to get your feel-good snacking on, we have just the delicious snack for you.

 

What's in each Fruit Bar? Only the best stuff!

- Real fruit or juice

- No GMO ingredients*

- Excellent or good source of Vitamin C**

- No artificial colors or flavors (added colors from natural sources)

- Gluten-free
