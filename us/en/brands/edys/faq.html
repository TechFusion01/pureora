FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Expand your knowledge of Edy's ice cream

Our Edy’s 1.5qt containers do not have a tamper seal. We have very strict quality control, including inspection of incoming ingredients and finished products. Quality and Safety for our consumers is very important to us.

We do not offer dairy free Edy's products at this time.

We are 100% confident about the safety of Dreyer's/Edy’s® Ice Cream and the rest of our ice cream family of products. Delivery of safe, high quality products is one of our core principles at Dreyer's Grand Iced Cream Inc. and we guarantee all our products are manufactured to be compliant with FDA requirements at the time of delivery to our retail customers. Have a specific question? Give us a call at (877) 437–3937.

The Edy’s® ice cream flavor team works year-round experimenting with new flavor ideas or modifications to flavors we already offer. While we cannot guarantee that your request for a new ice cream flavor will be acted upon, we can assure you that we will pass along your suggestion to our flavor team for their consideration.

Dreyer's and Edy’s® proudly market our products under Dreyer's Ice Cream west of the Rocky Mountains and in Texas. On the other side of the nation, we proudly market our products under Edy’s® Ice Cream in the East. We do this to honor both of our founders, William Dreyer and Joseph Edy as well as our past.

From time to time we need to review our Edy's ice cream flavor portfolio and discontinue flavors for various reasons. Please use our Where To Buy tool to help you discover other options with similar ingredients. We do like to hear which flavors are favorites so we welcome your comments.

Edy’s® takes extra care to make premium quality ice cream and a frozen snack for our customers and it is a real pleasure when consumers take the time to confirm that the extra effort is worthwhile. We are delighted to hear that you enjoyed your recent purchase. Our flavor development team members work very hard each year improving tried and true favorites and creating new and exciting flavor combinations -- they will be happy to receive your positive feedback! Leave a review here on our website, or you can also become a fan of Edy’s® brand on Facebook to share your thoughts on our page or leave us a review about your favorite product on our website.

One recommended serving of Edy’s® ice cream is equal to 2/3 cup. Take your ice cream serving one step further by pairing it with healthy toppings like fruit or nuts. Learn more.

The caffeine amounts in Edy's ice cream varies, particularly with flavors that contain chocolate or coffee. It typically ranges from 0 - 25 milligrams per 2/3 cup.

As a general rule, the gluten in Edy's frozen dessert products is present only in the added bakery products, such as cookies, cake or brownies. We always label the eight major food allergens on our package by their common name. We recommend to always check the label for the most current information before purchasing and/or consuming a product. The exception to this rule is our Slow Churned French Silk ice cream, which contains gluten in the natural flavors.

At Edy's, we always label the eight major food allergens, including peanuts, on our package by their common name. If an allergen does not appear in the ingredient statement, then it has not been added to the product. We recommend to always check the label for the most current information before purchasing and/or consuming a product.

At Edy's, we always label the eight major food allergens, including eggs, on our package by their common name. If an allergen does not appear in the ingredient statement, then it has not been added to the product. We recommend to always check the label for the most current information before purchasing and/or consuming a product.

Edy's does not have dedicated peanut or tree nut free lines or facilities. Although, precautionary measures are taken to prevent cross contamination. These include: thorough cleaning of our production lines and equipment, and a strict separation of materials that contain the eight allergens.
