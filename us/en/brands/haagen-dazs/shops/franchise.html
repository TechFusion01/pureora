Why Häagen-Dazs

START BUILDING YOUR FUTURE. ONE SCOOP AT A TIME.

Owning a Häagen-Dazs ice cream shop franchise is so much more than just a delicious business venture. With Häagen-Dazs, you are part of something bigger. Our internationally renowned brand, world-class ice cream and ongoing franchisee training and support provides the opportunity to achieve great things from behind the counter. Using only the finest ingredients and best-in-class flavors, our ice cream creates a truly delightful dessert experience that has kept our guests coming back time and time again. Indulge in the moment and explore the opportunity to open a Häagen-Dazs franchise today.

WHY START AN ICE CREAM FRANCHISE?

If you’re thinking about taking advantage of a Häagen-Dazs franchise opportunity, you might be wondering what’s in it for you. There are significant advantages to franchising with Häagen-Dazs. Some of those advantages include:

A product with significant brand recognition

Ability to build a business foundation around a quality product

Exceptional support from your franchisor

A variety of store formats — find one to fit your location!

Marketing materials to all ice cream shop franchises for sales success

So many more!
