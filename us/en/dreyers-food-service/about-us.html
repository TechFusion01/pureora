ABOUT US

We Know Ice Cream

In 1928, ice cream maker William Dreyer and candy maker Joseph Edy opened an ice cream shop together in Oakland, California. They soon made history with deliciously inventive flavors like the original Rocky Road. Families gathered at the scoop shop for guaranteed good times and their favorite flavors.

Four generations later, we’re still devoted to making the richest, creamiest original family favorites. Making those everyday moments extra special for your family is just as important to us as it is to you. That’s why we craft sweet deliciousness into every ice cream scoop.The Dreyer's foodservce team will help you continue this tradition in your location by helping with menu innovation, solutions for delivery and drive thru and producst that make serving ice cream easy for your frontline crew.
