Chocolate Dipped
Watermelon
Superfood Bars

Not all heroes wear capes, but this recipe will have you feeling super!

Details

Servings

  1

Diffculty

  Easy

 

 

Time

Active Time

30 min

Inactive Time

1 hour

Total Time

90 min

Ingredients

4 Outshine® Watermelon Frozen Fruit Bars

1 cup dark chocolate chips

1 tablespoon coconut oil

2 tablespoons chia seeds

2 tablespoons coconut flakes

2 tablespoons cup cacao nibs

Instructions

Add the coconut oil and chocolate chips in a microwave safe bowl. Melt for 15-30 seconds in the microwave, or until the chocolate is smooth. Allow the chocolate to cool.

Dip or drizzle chocolate on 1/2 of each Outshine® Watermelon Frozen Fruit Bars. Set dipped bars on parchment paper.

Add a mixture of chia seeds, coconut flakes, and cacao nibs to each dipped bar. You may mix and match flavors to your taste!

Allow bars to set in the freezer and enjoy.
