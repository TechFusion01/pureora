Ice Cream
Dirt Parfait

Get your hands dirty with this fun homemade dessert activity. Dig in!

Details

Servings

  1

Diffculty

  Easy

 

 

Time

Active Time

15 min

Inactive Time

0 min

Total Time

15 min

Ingredients

Dreyer's Chocolate ice cream or Edy's® Chocolate ice cream

Chocolate sandwich cookies

Gummy worms

Instructions

Remove the creamy middle from the chocolate sandwich cookies. Crush up the cookies and set aside.

In a glass, sprinkle crushed cookie bits in a glass and top with a little chocolate ice cream.

Continue alternating cookie and ice cream layers until the glass is filled.

Top with a few gummy worms and dig in!
